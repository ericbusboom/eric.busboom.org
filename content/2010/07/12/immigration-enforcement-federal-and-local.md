![](http://www.sdoi.com/content/gsa/images/seal_immigration.jpg)The US Justice Department is [suing the state of Arizona ](<http://blogs.abcnews.com/politicalpunch/2010/07/justice-department-sues-arizona-for-immigration-law.html>)over [SB 1070](<http://www.azleg.gov/legtext/49leg/2r/bills/sb1070s.pdf>), because "[The Individual Sections of S.B. 1070 are Prempted by Federal Law](<http://abcnews.go.com/images/Politics/Arizona%20PI%20Brief.pdf>)". The theory, as I understand it, is that the Feds assert that only the Feds should be enforcing Federal Immigration law. The irony ( actually, one of many ) is that for the last 15 years, the Federal Homeland Security department has been running the [ICE 287(g) program](<http://www.ice.gov/pi/news/factsheets/section287_g.htm>), in which Immigration and Customs Enforcement trains local police to enforce federal law. The program has 9 signed Memorandums with Arizona police departments. So, it appears that one federal department is suing Arizona for doing what another federal department is training them to do.
